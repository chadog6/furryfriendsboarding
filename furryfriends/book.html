Boarding

Our boarding guests join our daycare crew during the day and play in our supervised and monitored play groups. We do kennel for feeding, and overnight.Boarding is charged per night. Drop-off can be scheduled any time during business hours. We strongly encourage morning-early afternoon drop-offs to allow for plenty of playtime before bedtime.
